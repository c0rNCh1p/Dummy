DESCRIPTION  : Vivaldi is an open source, cross platform web browser developed by Vivaldi Technologies, a company
   founded by Tatsuki Tomita and Jon Stephenson Von Tetzchner, who was the co-founder and CEO of Opera Software.
   Vivaldi was officially launched in April 2016. It is first and foremost targeted towards technically inclined
   users as well as former Opera users disgruntled by its transition from the Presto Layout Engine to a Chromium based
   browser that resulted in the loss of many of its iconic features. Despite being Opera based, Vivaldi aims to revive
   the features of the Presto Engine with more flexibility and efficiency than its predecessors.
