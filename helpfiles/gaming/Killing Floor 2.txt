DESCRIPTION
In Killing Floor 2, players descend into continental europe where the outbreak
caused by Horzine Biotechs failed experiment has quickly spread and gained
unstoppable momentum, essentially paralyzing the European Union. Just one month
after the events in the original Killing Floor, the specimen clones are
everywhere and civilization is in disarray. Communications have failed,
governments have collapsed, and military forces have been systematically
eradicated. The people of europe know survival and self preservation too well
and some lucky survivors have gone into hiding, not all have given up hope
though... A group of civilians and mercenaries have banded together to combat
the outbreak and established privately funded operation bases across europe.
Upon tracking specimen clone outbreaks, players will descend into zed-laden hot
zones and exterminate them. 6 player co-op Zed slaughtering mayhem and now, with
12 player Versus Survival Mode, BE the Zeds!
